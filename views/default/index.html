What is Onlineexam.com ?


Onlineexam.com is education portal. It consists online examinations which have multiple choice questions. Our portal offers IQ tests of different levels. IQ Level 1 is for beginners and IQ Level 2 for experts.


Online Exam Pattern

  • All questions of the Online Exam are of objective type (multiple choice questions with only one correct choice of answer).

  • Each correct answer fetches 1 mark, while each incorrect answer has zero marks.

  • You can look at them once you have signed up so register and get going. You dont need to complete IQ Level 1 in order to sit for IQ Level 2. Best Of luck !
